# Terminology Conflict Checklist

## Purpose
- Track terminology and rule-strength consistency checks for governed documents.

## Checklist
- [ ] Document path is listed.
- [ ] Strength expressions (`must` / `should` / `should not` / `must not`) are interpreted consistently with `../vocabulary-management_vocabulary-rule/SKILL.md`.
- [ ] Ambiguous terms are either removed or have explicit decision criteria.
- [ ] Single source of truth references are present.
- [ ] Conflict findings and resolution notes are recorded.

## Ownership
- Maintained by the designated document owner.
